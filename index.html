About

I'm a Computer Science PhD Student at Stony Brook University (soon to be Vanderbilt University) working with professor Prof. H Andrew Schwartz and the HLAB researching ideas at the intersection of psychology and AI. I hold a BS with a double major in Computer Science and Cybersecurity from Randolph-Macon College, with minors in math and ethics, and have been coding academically and professionally for over a decade.

My current interests are in how we can improve recommender systems to better target well-being using a variety of Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy painting/drawing, playing pickup volleyball, and evidently website design.
